Social media refers to online platforms and websites that allow users to create and share content, connect with others, and participate in virtual communities. It has become an integral part of our digital lives, enabling people to communicate, collaborate, and express themselves in various ways. Social media platforms like Facebook, TikTok, and Instagram provide users with the ability to share photos, videos, and thoughts, as well as interact with others through comments, likes, and messages. These platforms have revolutionized the way we connect with friends, family, and even strangers, making it easier to stay connected and informed in today's interconnected world.
